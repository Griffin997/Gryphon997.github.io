Three Card Monty

The rules are simple. You will be shown three cards; an ace of spades, a queen of spades, and an ace of clubs. The cards will then be turned over and will switch places with each other 10 times. Keep track of where the queen is. After 10 rotations, you will be asked to select the queen. When you are ready to begin, click start.
